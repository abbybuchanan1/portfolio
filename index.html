Research Perspective

A Hidden Layer of Work

Most digital systems require people to translate their thinking into structured formats.

Human cognition is nonlinear, contextual, and often ambiguous. Systems, however, demand clarity, sequence, and precision.

This mismatch creates an invisible layer of cognitive labor.

My work focuses on designing that layer—reducing friction between human intent and system requirements so people can move forward with less strain.
