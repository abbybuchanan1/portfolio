Scaffold

A system for reducing overwhelm and supporting task initiation.

The Problem

Many users don’t struggle with knowing what to do — they struggle with starting.

Design Approach

Scaffold acts as a cognitive prosthetic — externalizing executive function at the moment it is needed most.

Focus Mode

The interface removes all unnecessary elements, leaving only the task and a rhythmic visual timer.

Why it works

It creates a bounded container for attention, reducing overwhelm and making action possible.
