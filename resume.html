Summary

UX researcher and strategist focused on how people experience systems under real-world conditions: cognitively, emotionally, and sensorily. My work centers on moments where people get stuck—overwhelm, ambiguity, breakdown in task initiation—and on translating behavioral insight into clearer, more usable systems.

I’m especially interested in how AI and emerging technologies can reduce cognitive burden, support decision-making, and expand who is able to participate effectively within complex systems.
