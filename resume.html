Summary

Research, systems, and human behavior

My work focuses on moments where people get stuck—overwhelm, ambiguity, and breakdown in task initiation—and translating behavioral insight into clearer, more usable systems.

I’m particularly interested in how individuals interact within larger systems—how differences in cognition, behavior, and communication shape outcomes across teams, environments, and tools.

I’m interested in contributing this work within a collaborative product environment, supporting research-driven teams working on complex, high-impact systems.
